In brief:
An easy under 2-mile loop through Sutro's woods in the middle of the City.

Distance, category, and difficulty:
This 1.8 mile loop hike is easy.

Exposure:
Mostly shaded.

Trail traffic:
Light.

Trail surfaces:
Dirt and paved trails.

Hiking time:
1 hour.

Season:
Nice any time; usually muddy in winter.

Getting there:

Mount Sutro is nearly in the center of San Francisco -- from Laguna Honda Boulevard turn east onto Clarendon and drive uphill to side of street parking near the intersection with Johnstone Drive.

GPS Coordinates* for Trailhead:
Latitude 37°45'29.40"N
Longitude 122°27'10.27"W
(* based on Google Earth data, shown as degrees, minutes, seconds)

Gas, food, and lodging:
Gas, pay phones, stores, and restaurants in surrounding neighborhoods. No camping.

Trailhead details:
Side of street parking. No designated handicapped spots. No restrooms or drinking water. No parking or entrance fees. The trails are not suitable to wheelchairs. There are no paper maps offered, but the hike is well-signed. This trailhead is within walking distance of SF MUNI bus and trolley lines. Read some cautions about urban hikes.

Rules:
No rules are posted.
